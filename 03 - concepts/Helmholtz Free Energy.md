The Helmholtz free energy $F$ is the [[State Function|state function]]

$$
F=E-TS,
$$

where $E$ is [[Internal Energy|internal energy]] and $S$ is [[Entropy|entropy]]. Its natural variables are temperature and the [[Generalized Displacements|generalized displacements]], and it decreases during isothermal relaxation without mechanical work.

First introduced in [[1.7 Approach to Equilibrium and the Thermodynamic Potentials]].
