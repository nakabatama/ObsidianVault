A Carnot cycle is the cycle followed by a [[Carnot Engine|Carnot engine]].

It is constructed from two isothermal processes and two [[Reversible Transformation|reversible]] [[Adiabatic Transformation|adiabatic processes]].

The two isothermal processes occur at temperatures $T_H$ and $T_C$.

First introduced at [[Kardar - 1.5 Carnot Engines]].
