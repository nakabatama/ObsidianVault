Heat capacities measure the change in temperature when heat is supplied to a system.

Since heat is not a [[State Function|state function]], the path must be specified. The two common choices are constant volume and constant pressure:

$$
C_V=\left(\frac{\mathrm{\bar{d}}Q}{\mathrm{d}T}\right)_V,
$$
$$
C_P=\left(\frac{\mathrm{\bar{d}}Q}{\mathrm{d}T}\right)_P.
$$

First introduced at [[Kardar - 1.3 The First Law]].
