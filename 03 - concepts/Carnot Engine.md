A Carnot engine is a [[Reversible Transformation|reversible]] engine that runs in a [[Cyclic Transformation|cycle]] and exchanges heat only with a source at temperature $T_H$ and a sink at temperature $T_C$.

Its distinguishing feature is that all heat exchanges take place at two fixed temperatures.

A Carnot engine can be run backward as a [[Idealized Refrigerator|refrigerator]].

First introduced at [[Kardar - 1.5 Carnot Engines]].
