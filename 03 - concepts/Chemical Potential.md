The chemical potential $\mu_i$ is the energetic cost of adding one particle of species $i$ under specified thermodynamic conditions.

It is conjugate to the particle number $N_i$ and enters the [[Chemical Work|chemical work]] as

$$
\mathrm{\bar{d}}W_N=\sum_i\mu_i\,\mathrm{d}N_i.
$$

First introduced in [[1.7 Approach to Equilibrium and the Thermodynamic Potentials]].
