The first law of thermodynamics expresses conservation of energy in a thermodynamic process.

Heat supplied to a system changes its [[Internal Energy|internal energy]] and contributes to work done by or on the system.

It rules out [[Perpetual Motion Machines of the First Kind|perpetual motion machines of the first kind]].

First introduced at [[Kardar - 1.3 The First Law]].
