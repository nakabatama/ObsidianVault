Generalized forces are the intensive variables conjugate to [[Generalized Displacements|generalized displacements]].

Examples include pressure for volume, surface tension for area, tension for length, and electric field for polarization.

In a [[Quasi-static Transformation|quasi-static transformation]], they enter the work term as

$$
\mathrm{\bar{d}}W=\sum_iJ_i\mathrm{d}x_i.
$$

First introduced at [[Kardar - 1.3 The First Law]].
