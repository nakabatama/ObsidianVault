Generalized displacements are the extensive variables used to describe mechanical or other external changes of a thermodynamic system.

Examples include volume for a fluid, area for a film, length for a wire, and polarization for a dielectric.

In a [[Quasi-static Transformation|quasi-static transformation]], they are paired with [[Generalized Forces|generalized forces]] in the work term:

$$
\mathrm{\bar{d}}W=\sum_iJ_i\mathrm{d}x_i.
$$
