The isothermal compressibility describes the relative change in volume of a system in response to a change in pressure at fixed temperature.

It is defined as

$$
\kappa_{T}=-\frac{1}{V}\left(\frac{\partial V}{\partial P}\right)_{T}.
$$

It is a [[Response Functions|response function]].

First introduced at [[Kardar - 1.3 The First Law]].
